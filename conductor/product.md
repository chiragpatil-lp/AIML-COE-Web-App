# Product Guide - AIML COE Web Application

## Vision
The AIML COE Web Application serves as the definitive master entry point for the AI/ML Center of Excellence. It is designed to bridge the gap between complex AI/ML capabilities and the stakeholders who need them, providing a centralized hub for knowledge, resources, and administrative control.

## Target Audience
- **Organization Employees:** Seeking AI/ML resources, tools, and expertise to enhance their workflows.
- **Managers:** Looking to understand COE pillars and how AI/ML can be integrated into their business units.
- **Potential Customers:** Interested in exploring the COE's capabilities through showcased case studies and successful implementations.
- **Administrators:** COE team members responsible for content management, user access control, and platform monitoring.

## Core Objectives
- **Centralize Knowledge:** Act as a single source of truth for all AI/ML COE pillars and resources.
- **Demonstrate Value:** Showcase high-impact case studies to build trust and attract internal and external partnerships.
- **Streamline Administration:** Provide a robust dashboard for managing users, permissions, and platform content.
- **Promote Engagement:** Facilitate continuous learning and updates through integrated blogs, newsletters, and news sections.

## Key Features
- **Pillar Directory:** A comprehensive, searchable repository of AI/ML COE core pillars and resources.
- **Content Hub:** Integrated hosting for blogs, case studies, and newsletters with modern reading experiences.
- **Admin Dashboard:** Full-featured management suite for user administration, RBAC configuration, and content moderation.
- **Security & Access:** Secure authentication flow with granular role-based access control.
- **Interactive UI:** A highly responsive and engaging interface featuring animations and 3D graphics to reflect the cutting-edge nature of AI/ML.

## Success Metrics
- High adoption rate among internal employees.
- Increased engagement with case studies and newsletters.
- Efficient administrative workflows for user and content management.
